Frequently Asked Questions

Legal translation requires specialized knowledge of legal terminology, concepts, and systems in both source and target languages. Our translators are not just language experts but also have legal training to ensure precise, contextually accurate translations that maintain the legal validity of your documents.

We implement strict confidentiality protocols including NDAs with all staff, secure file transfer systems, and encrypted storage. You can also request additional security measures for particularly sensitive materials.

We specialize in two core services with exceptional accuracy:

1. Hindi to English Legal Translations:
- Certified translations of legal documents (contracts, affidavits, court orders)
- Business document translation (agreements, financial reports)
- Personal document conversion (certificates, diplomas, IDs)
- 100% human translation with legal review

2. Handwritten to Digital Conversion:
- Accurate typing of English handwritten documents
- Preserving original formatting and layout
- Special handling of difficult handwriting
- Fast turnaround with 99.5% accuracy guarantee

Our focused expertise ensures you get the most precise translations and conversions, backed by rigorous quality checks.
